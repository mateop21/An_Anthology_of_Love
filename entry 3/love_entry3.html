Hope

A very joyful and loveable dog



An image of my wonderful and energetic dog. My family decided to adopt a dog about five years ago during a cold February Winter. Nothing could prepare us for the journey we were about to embark on. Hope has helped bring my family together in a moment when it felt like everyone was going on their own paths. She has taught us to be more loving and delicate. Hope has comforted us by simply being herself.












Love means never giving up. Love is an adaptable language with no boundries. Love is comforting.

Back at home during cold winters and heavy blizzards my sister and I would always anticipate the arrival of snow. A lot of snow. Before going out as we would suit up to go outside our imagination would run wild with the countless stories of what the outside world could bring. I loved putting on my snow boots and all my other "tactical" equipment and pretend I was Han Solo about to go on a search in Hoth. My sister and I would gather the icicles around my house and pretend they were precious crystals. In addition I always loved the idea of building an inhabitable space for us and our icicles. As my sister would go searching for the perfect icicles, I would spend hours building the perfect igloo. Sometimes this activity would take us days. We would wait for the icicles to grow and for more snow to fall to help build on our igloo foundations. After a long day of working on our "research and exploration" we would go back home just before it got dark and we would drink hot chocolate. These are a few of countless of fond memories I have with my sister.

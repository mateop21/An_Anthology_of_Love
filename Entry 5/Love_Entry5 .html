Throughout the past year I have placed an emphasis on documenting and keeping track
of my ideas and moments that happen in my life. It can be very simple as a word, letter
or color that has captured my attention and made me think of another idea, or even sparked
creativity in me. It can also be a conversation between friends or strangers. It can be the
name of an artist or a piece of work that I want to review. It can also just be phrases that help
me keep going. Sometimes it can just be reminders for the day such as to pick up eggs before
going home or anything else. No matter how random or insignificant it may be, it is a
documentation and reflection of who I am as a person in that moment in time. Sometimes it
helps to look back and see what I was thinking or what was going on in my life.

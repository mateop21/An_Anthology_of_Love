An Anthology of Love


The following is a working collection centered around the concept of love.
I would like to encourage everyone to think of what love means to them.
How does it feel? Who makes you feel like this? What do you love? Why do you love?
Are there different forms of love and if so what differentiates one from the other?
How do you know what love is? How do you know what it isn't? How does it feel to be loved?
These are questions that we must ask ourselves, especially as we constanly traverse
through different realities on a daily basis centered around consumerism and superficiality.
In a world where you're worthless if you don't consume, how can we define emotions and thoughts
that represent something more, something bigger than ourselves.

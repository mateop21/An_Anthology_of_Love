An Anthology of Love


The following is a working collection centered around the concept of love. I would like to encourage everyone to think of what love means to them. How does it feel? Who makes you feel like this? What do you love? Why do you love? How do you know what love is? How do you know what it isn't? What does it feel to be loved? These are questions that we must ask ourselves, especially as we constanly traverse from our reality to a virutal one on a daily basis centered around consumerism and superficiality. In a world where you're worthless if you don't consume.
